Mathematical Model

We can model the interaction between two oscillators \(i\) and \(j\) using the Kuramoto model. The phase \( \theta_i \) of oscillator \(i\) evolves according to the differential equation:

\[ \frac{d\theta_i}{dt} = \omega_i + \frac{K}{N} \sum_{j=1}^{N} \sin(\theta_j - \theta_i) \]

Here, \( \omega_i \) is the natural frequency of oscillator \(i\), \( K \) is the coupling strength, and the sum is over all \( N \) oscillators. This equation shows how each firefly adjusts its phase based on the phase differences with others.
